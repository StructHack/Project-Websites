About US

We are a team of young, enthusiast and like minded people working together through our expertise in various domains of information security for a common goal of securing the Internet cyber space.

Services

Vulnerability Assessment

Identifying vulnerability and classifying it is one of the most important step towards making something more secure.So, we identify and classify the found vulnerability in web applications and mobile applications and provide necessary information about the vulnerability so the company doing assessment understands the threats and can react properly.

Penetration Testing

We perform both manual and automated testing to find the vulnearble services, endpoints, and try exploiting the found vulnerability to gain access to the system before some malicious user does so.

Incident Management

Getting hacked is sometimes inevitable and there can be a lot of questions after the incident. We help answering your questions. We also help you find the root cause of the hack and eradicate it in an efficient and budget friendly way.

Security Training and Consultant

We also provide security consultation and training to companies and its employees to better manage their security. Training employees is one of the most important aspect on securing the business from malicious users. Making employees aware about attacks like Social Engineering can be invaluable.
